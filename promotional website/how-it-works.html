🌿 How PlantPal Works

Plant care made effortless — learn how our smart planter keeps your greenery thriving. 🌞💧

1️⃣

📦 Unbox & Set Up

Plug in PlantPal, fill the water reservoir, and place your plant. Download the mobile app to begin setup.

2️⃣

📶 Connect to Wi-Fi

Use the PlantPal app to connect your device to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth and sync it with your account.

3️⃣

🛰️ Sensor Monitoring

PlantPal continuously checks soil moisture, light, humidity, and temperature to learn your plant’s needs.

4️⃣

💧 Smart Watering

Based on the sensor data, it waters your plant only when necessary — no more overwatering!

5️⃣

🔔 Get Notifications

Receive alerts for low water levels, ideal light positioning, and care tips through the app.

6️⃣

📊 Track Progress

Monitor growth and health trends over time and get personalized advice for optimal care.

🤖🌱 Technology Meets Nature

PlantPal combines cutting-edge sensor technology, automation, and mobile connectivity to simplify your plant care routine. All while using eco-conscious materials and low-energy design to protect the planet. 🌍

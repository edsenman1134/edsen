What is PlantPal?

PlantPal is a smart planter system that automatically monitors and nurtures your plants using real-time data. Whether you're a busy professional, a beginner gardener, or simply forgetful, PlantPal makes plant care easy and enjoyable.

  • Automatic watering with moisture sensors
  • Light and temperature tracking
  • Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Mobile app for alerts and tips

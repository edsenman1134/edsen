Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

🌱 What is PlantPal?

PlantPal is a smart plant care assistant that helps you monitor, water, and take care of your plants using AI-powered suggestions and reminders.

📱 Do I need to download an app?

No download required. PlantPal works directly from your browser and syncs with your PlantPal device if you have one.

💧 How does the watering system work?

The smart watering system detects soil moisture and automatically waters your plant based on its specific needs.

🛠️ Is it hard to install?

Not at all! It takes just a few minutes to set up. Plug in the sensor, connect to Wi-Fi, and you're ready to go.

💳 What are the payment options?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and GCash. Subscription options are available monthly or annually.
